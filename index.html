About

Biography

I work on multimodal large language models, reinforcement learning for LLMs, autonomous agent systems, and machine learning, with a broader interest in building more capable and reliable AI systems.

My recent projects span open-vocabulary detection, tool-using multimodal agents, autonomous skill generation, and improving RL training efficiency for large language models.

I am especially interested in research that connects strong empirical performance with practical usability, interpretability, and real-world deployment.
